Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Slice and quarter mushrooms

    4 min

    While it heats up, slice half of the chestnut mushrooms and cut the other half into quarters to add texture.

    Equipment: knife, cutting board

  2. Take out pans

    1 min

    take out two saucepans and a frying pan.

    Equipment: saucepan, frying pan

  3. Peel and cut potatoes

    5 min

    Peel the potatoes, then cut into 2cm cubes.

    Equipment: peeler, knife, cutting board

  4. Rinse vegetables

    2 min

    rinse any raw grains, herbs or vegetables.

  5. Boil kettle

    3 min

    Boil your kettle

    Equipment: kettle

  6. Heat frying pan with oil

    2 min

    Place your frying pan over a medium heat and add a drizzle of oil.

    Equipment: frying pan, hob

  7. Fill saucepans with boiling water

    1 min

    Fill both of your saucepans with boiling water from your kettle

    Equipment: saucepan, kettle

  8. Fry mushrooms until golden

    10 min

    Fry the mushrooms until golden and caramelised, around 10 minutes

    Equipment: frying pan, spatula

  9. Place saucepans over medium heat

    1 min

    then place over a medium heat.

    Equipment: hob, saucepan

  10. Cook bourguignon sauce with mushrooms

    3 min

    then add the bourguignon sauce and cook for a further 2-3 minutes or until piping hot. Add water as needed if the sauce reduces too much or starts to stick.

    Equipment: frying pan, spatula

  11. Boil potatoes until very soft

    11 min

    Salt your boiling water, then add the potatoes to the water and cook for around 10-12 minutes or until the potato is very soft. It should virtually crumble when you jab it with a fork.

    Equipment: saucepan, fork

  12. Boil kale until al dente

    8 min

    Place the kale in your other saucepan and boil for 5-10 minutes or until al dente.

    Equipment: saucepan

  13. Season bourguignon sauce

    1 min

    Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.

  14. Mash potatoes until smooth

    3 min

    Once the potatoes are cooked, add a generous drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, then mash until smooth and creamy. You could also add a little plant milk or butter to make it even creamier (optional).

    Equipment: potato masher, saucepan

  15. Season mashed potatoes

    1 min

    Season well with salt and black pepper to taste.

  16. Plate mash, bourguignon, and kale

    2 min

    To serve up, divide the mash between serving bowls, ladle over the mushroom bourguignon and serve alongside the kale.

    Equipment: serving bowls, ladle
